Delete Account – BTC Miner Simulator

This page explains how users of the BTC Miner Simulator app can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly from inside the app by following these steps:

  1. Open the BTC Miner Simulator app.
  2. Go to the Settings section.
  3. Select "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm the deletion request.

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User account information (email and user ID)
  • Mining progress and virtual rewards
  • Referral data
  • App activity linked to the account

Data Retention

Some data may be temporarily retained for security or fraud prevention purposes. Any retained data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.
